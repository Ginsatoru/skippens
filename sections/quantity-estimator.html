General Information

  • All quantities are estimates only and are based on the information provided
  • Please ensure measurements are entered in the correct units
  • Results are rounded up for practical application
  • The estimator is not intended for very small areas
  • Values under 0.01 may be inaccurate due to rounding

Bulk Items

  • Product densities can vary, so exact quantities cannot be guaranteed
  • Consider whether it is better to have slightly more or less material
  • Excess material may need to be reused or disposed of responsibly
  • Shortages may require an additional delivery fee
  • Always allow for compaction and settling of materials

Turf & Pavers

Turf Installation:

  • Allow at least 5% extra for cutting and wastage
  • Quantities are rounded up to full rolls
  • Requirements may vary based on installation technique
  • Consider site slope and irregular shapes

Pavers Installation:

  • Cutting may be required, increasing material wastage
  • Use area dimensions that match paver size multiples
  • Include allowance for edge restraints and base materials
  • Complex patterns require additional material
